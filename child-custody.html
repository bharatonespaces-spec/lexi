Overview

Child custody is often the most emotionally challenging aspect of separation. At Lexi Services, we approach these cases with extreme sensitivity. We understand that while your marriage may be ending, your role as parents continues forever.


We advocate for sustainable custody arrangements that ensure the child's emotional and physical well-being while protecting your rights to access, visitation, and decision-making.

Key Areas We Cover

Physical Custody

Determining who the child lives with primarily and visitation schedules for the non-custodial parent.

Legal Custody

Decision-making rights regarding the child's education, healthcare, and religious upbringing.

Child Maintenance

Fair calculation of financial support to ensure the child's lifestyle is maintained.
