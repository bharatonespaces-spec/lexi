Overview

When both spouses mutually agree to separate, Mutual Consent Divorce is the most dignified and efficient legal path. Under Section 13B of the Hindu Marriage Act (or corresponding sections in other acts), this process respects the decision of the couple to part ways amicably.


At Lexi Services, we prioritize reducing emotional stress. We handle the drafting, filing, and coordination, so you can focus on moving forward.

Step-by-Step Process

Step 1: Joint Petition Drafting

We draft a comprehensive petition covering alimony, custody, and asset division terms.

Step 2: First Motion Hearing

Both parties appear in court to record statements. The 6-month cooling-off period begins.

Step 3: Second Motion Hearing

After the cooling period, parties confirm the decision. The court grants the final decree.
