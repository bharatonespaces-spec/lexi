Overview

Cooperative Housing Societies (CHS) function under complex bylaws and regulations. We provide end-to-end legal support for societies to manage their affairs legally and for members to protect their rights.

Our Services

Deemed Conveyance

Helping societies obtain legal ownership of the land from the builder/developer through the Deemed Conveyance process.

Recovery of Dues

Legal action under Section 101 for recovery of maintenance arrears from defaulting members without going to civil court.

Parking & Leakage Disputes

Resolving common disputes between members or between members and the committee regarding parking allotment and leakage repairs.
