Overview

A Marriage Certificate is the only legal proof of marriage in India. Whether you are planning a Court Marriage or need to register an existing religious marriage, we streamline the bureaucratic process for you.


We handle everything from filing the "Notice of Intended Marriage" to organizing the final registration day, ensuring all documents are perfect to avoid rejections.

Step-by-Step Process

Step 1: Notice Filing

Submitting the intended marriage notice to the Registrar. 30-day objection period begins.

Step 2: Verification

Documents are verified by the officer. We ensure everything is in order before this stage.

Step 3: Registration

On the 31st day (or appointed date), couple + 3 witnesses sign the register. Certificate issued.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate / School Leaving (Age Proof)
  • Aadhaar Card / Passport (Address Proof)
  • Passport Photos (3 each)
  • 3 Witnesses (with ID & Address Proof)
